About me

My other skills include proficient writing and speaking skills in English, intermediate level Dutch, willingness to learn new skills, teamwork, problem solving, adaptability, and dedication. I am also a certified Scrum Master with experience in an Agile/Scrum environment.

Graduated with honours from the University College Maastricht with a Bachelors in Arts. During my time in university, I focused primarily on Political Studies and minored in Sociology. However, gradually I felt more interested in the technicalogical industry and a career path. I began to teach myself to program in HTML and CSS, and then studied in the New York Design and Coding Academy for three months in their Amsterdam based school. Afterwards, I began a 6-month traineeship at IBM CIC and have since been working as a full time employee building internal web applications. My programming skills include: HTML5, CSS, SASS, JavaScript (ES5/ES6), Vue.js, Pug/Jade, Node.js, Express.js, Axios, Swift, & Git & Github.
